Solve the charts

So many people, especially traders, jump head-first into their trades without a clear vision of what they want to accomplish. That's bad. Not only will you lose money, but you will end up not understanding the essence of making money in the stock market.

Instead your trades should be executed in a systematic manner, without emotion, and focused on the charts and the method. After all, you don't want to use the stock market as a casino.
